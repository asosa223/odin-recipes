Pan Seared Chicken

A beautiful pan seared chicken

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Butter
  • Rosemary
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Season chicken with salt and pepper
  2. Heat pan on medium-high heat
  3. Oil pan and place chicken gently inside of it
  4. Flip after 2-3 mins and cok through until 165 internal temp
